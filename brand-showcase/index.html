Frequently Asked Questions

What is Franchise KI Brand Showcase?
It is the Franchise KI growth path for franchise brands that want to claim or improve their listing, verify data, increase visibility, and ask about featured placement or lead handling support.
Is this for franchisors or franchise buyers?
This page is for franchise brands and franchisor teams. Franchise buyers should use the public Franchise KI search and PerfectFit AI experience.
Can we update our brand data?
Yes. The first step is to request access or a review so your key brand details can be checked and updated. Once your claim is verified, your team controls the profile.
How does featured placement work?
Featured placement helps approved franchise brands appear more prominently in relevant Franchise KI discovery experiences. Placement inventory is limited per category and market — submit the form and the team will walk you through current availability, eligibility, and pricing for your brand.
Can Franchise KI work leads for us?
You can request broker-led lead support. Availability, eligibility, and service levels are confirmed during your brand review, so the answer is specific to your brand rather than a one-size-fits-all package.
Do you guarantee franchise sales?
No. Franchise KI does not guarantee franchise sales, awards, approvals, candidate quality, or specific lead volume. What we improve is visibility and data quality where active candidates are already searching.
What happens after we submit the form?
The Franchise KI team reviews your brand, your current listing status, and your selected interest. From there, the team routes you toward listing cleanup, featured placement details, or a broker-led support conversation.
